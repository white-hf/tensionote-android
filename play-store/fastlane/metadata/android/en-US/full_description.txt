Tensionote is a focused blood pressure tracker built for daily use.

Record your systolic pressure, diastolic pressure, and heart rate quickly, then review a clear 2-week trend without digging through complex menus.

Key features:
- Quick entry from the home screen
- Detailed entry with optional tags and notes
- 2-week trend chart with direct high-pressure labels
- Full history with edit and delete
- Daily reminders
- PDF report export
- Email and file sharing for doctor visits
- English, Simplified Chinese, and Hindi

Tensionote is designed as a personal tracking and management tool. It does not diagnose disease or replace professional medical advice.
