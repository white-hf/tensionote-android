# Tensionote Android Support Contact Template

Replace the placeholders below before publishing to Google Play.

## Support Email

- Placeholder: `support@YOUR_DOMAIN`
- Recommended use: a mailbox monitored by the Android release owner or product owner

## Support Website

- Placeholder: `https://YOUR_DOMAIN/support`
- Recommended use: a simple page with:
  - app name
  - version
  - support email
  - privacy policy link
  - issue reporting instructions

## Privacy Policy Contact Section

Use the same support contact in the privacy policy:

- Email: `support@YOUR_DOMAIN`
- Website: `https://YOUR_DOMAIN/support`

## Before You Publish

- Verify the mailbox is reachable.
- Verify the support page is public.
- Verify the support contact matches the Play Console listing exactly.
