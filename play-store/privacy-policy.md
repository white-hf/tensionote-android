# Tensionote Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-03-31_

Tensionote is a blood pressure tracking application designed to help users record measurements, review trends, set reminders, and export reports from their Android device.

## Information We Store

In the current version of Tensionote, the app stores the following information locally on your device:

- Blood pressure records
- Heart rate values entered with blood pressure records
- Measurement time, tags, and optional notes
- Reminder schedules
- Exported PDF reports created by the app
- App language preference

## What We Do Not Do

In the current version, Tensionote does not:

- create a user account
- upload your health data to a remote server
- sell your personal information
- use analytics or advertising SDKs
- connect to Apple Health, Health Connect, or Bluetooth devices

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, Tensionote can send local reminder notifications on your device to remind you to record your blood pressure. Notification schedules are stored locally.

## File Sharing and Email

When you export a report and choose to share it by email or another app, the selected report file is passed to the app you choose. Tensionote does not control how third-party apps handle shared files.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, uninstall the app, or manually remove exported files.

## Medical Disclaimer

Tensionote is a personal health-management tool and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice. If you have concerns about your blood pressure or symptoms, consult a licensed medical professional.
