# Tensionote Brand Guidelines

## Selected Direction

`Concept C` is the approved direction.

## Core Brand Idea

- Blood drop:
  shows the health-recording focus immediately.
- Pulse line:
  signals measurement and trend insight.
- Dark teal container:
  makes the mark feel more premium and more stable than a light health-app default.
- Coral red drop:
  keeps urgency and medical relevance without becoming aggressive.

## Primary Assets

- Horizontal logo:
  `tensionote-logo-horizontal.svg`
- Icon-only mark:
  `tensionote-logo-icon.svg`
- Monochrome mark:
  `tensionote-logo-monochrome.svg`
- Dark-background lockup:
  `tensionote-logo-dark.svg`

## Recommended Colors

- Primary dark:
  `#12343B`
- Accent teal:
  `#177E89`
- Alert coral:
  `#E65555`
- Light surface:
  `#F5FAFA`
- Soft background:
  `#EAF5F4`

## Recommended Usage

- App icon:
  use the icon-only mark.
- Store listing feature graphic:
  use the horizontal lockup.
- Splash / onboarding:
  use the dark-background lockup.
- PDF / monochrome print:
  use the monochrome lockup.

## Avoid

- Do not stretch the icon container.
- Do not recolor the blood drop to blue or green.
- Do not place the default horizontal mark on busy photo backgrounds.
- Do not remove the wave line from the icon-only version.
