# Tensionote Logo Review

This folder contains the first-pass concepts and the approved follow-up brand package.

## Files

- `logo-concept-a.svg`
- `logo-concept-b.svg`
- `logo-concept-c.svg`

## Direction Summary

### Concept A

- Best fit if the product should feel like a clean, practical health tool.
- Strongest continuity with the current launcher icon.
- Safest option for app stores and onboarding screens.

### Concept B

- Softer and more consumer-facing.
- Better if the brand should feel calmer and less clinical.
- Slightly weaker than A/C at very small icon-like sizes.

### Concept C

- Most distinctive and premium.
- Better contrast at small sizes and better shelf impact in store listings.
- Strong candidate if you want the brand to feel more intentional and modern.

## Selected Direction

`Concept C` has been selected.

Reason:

- It keeps the blood-drop + wave language already used in the app icon.
- It has the best contrast.
- It is the most recognizable at app-store scale.
- It supports premium-looking store graphics without losing health-tool clarity.
