# Asset Export Guide

Use the SVG source files in this directory to export Play Store upload assets.

## App Icon

- Source: `icon/tensionote-app-icon.svg`
- Export size: `512x512`
- Format: `PNG`
- Keep the icon centered with generous outer padding.

## Feature Graphic

- Source: `feature-graphic/tensionote-feature-graphic.svg`
- Export size: `1024x500`
- Format: `PNG` or `JPG`

## Phone Screenshots

- Sources:
  - `screenshots/01-home-quick-entry.svg`
  - `screenshots/02-detailed-entry.svg`
  - `screenshots/03-history.svg`
  - `screenshots/04-trend.svg`
  - `screenshots/05-report.svg`
- Export size: `1080x1920`
- Format: `PNG` or `JPG`

## Suggested Final Screenshot Order

1. Quick entry
2. Detailed entry
3. History
4. Trend
5. Report export
